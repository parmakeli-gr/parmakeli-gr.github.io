Helen Parmakeli CV

Helen Parmakeli was born in 1975 in Athens Greece, where she lives and works. In 1997 she was admitted at the Fine Arts School of Athens. She studied painting, engraving and mosaic techniques at the Schools workshop. She excelled and graduated in 2003.
